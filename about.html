About Us

OurWok is a food delivery website that aims to reduce the carbon footprint by a margin. In today's world, people from the the same location place multiple orders. OurWok finds the people who are in the neighbourhood who are ordering from the same place and "mass orders" from the place thereby reducing the fuel emission. The pie chart shows that transportation is the leading cause for carbon emission. Since OurWok targets at neighbourhood restaurants and cafes, it allows delivery persons to walk to their destinations rather than drive. It also promotes employement among users who do not have a vehicle.
